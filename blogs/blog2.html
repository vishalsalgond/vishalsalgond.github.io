The Complete Python BootCamp 2021 - Udemy Course Review

Category: Education

David Lee | Seattle
Last updated 7 mins ago

My review of one of the best Udemy courses to learn Python for beginners — The Complete Python Bootcamp — Go from Zero to Hero by Jose Portilla on Udemy.

Image Courtesy: Udemy

1. The Instructor Reputation

Jose Portilla is an amazing teacher, trainer, and one of the most successful and famous instructors at the udemy platform with almost 2 million student enrollment. He has a bachelor’s and master’s degree from Santa Clara University in the Mechanical Engineering field and has years of experience in Data Science and Python language as well as web development such as HTML, CSS, Django, and AI. Currently he works as the Head of Data Science for Pierian Data Inc. and provides in-person data science and python programming training courses to employees working at top companies, including General Electric, Cigna, The New York Times, Credit Suisse, McKinsey, and many more. He is also one of the most popular Udemy instructors with millions of students and popular courses like SQL BootCamp and Python for Data Science course on Udemy.

2. The Course Structure and Content Quality

The course is very comprehensive, well structured, and focuses on teaching you Python Programming language as a beginner to be an advanced level in coding starting by setup the environment until you create some advanced programs such as web scrappers and email sender.
