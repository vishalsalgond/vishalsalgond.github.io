Apple iPhone 13 review: Almost like iPhone 12?

Category: Electronics

Vishal Salgond | Mumbai
Last updated 3 mins ago

The Apple iPhone is one of the leading product in smartphone market, yesterday Apple revealed their all new iPhones which kind of look and feel similar to previous generation of iPhones.

Image Courtesy: MacRumors

Like every year, this year also in the first half of the September Apple introduced the all new iPhones with slightly bigger screens, faster speeds and better cameras but to be honest there were no new major changes. In a prerecorded video of the event, Apple executives claimed the improvements in the new iPhone 13 as significant innovations, but they result in a device that looks and performs much like the iPhone 12 that Apple launched last year. Apple promised that the new iPhones will have a much more brighter screen, a longer battery life and much more powerful camera and processors. Having already pushed the screen nearly to the edge of the device, Apple slightly increased the size of the new iPhone 13 by shrinking down the huge notch at the top of the screen. Apple kept the same flat-edge design of the phone that it has used in other recent models.

Apple hopes that by adding these so called new features and making some slight design improvements, customers will still keep throwing extra money. Though this strategy of apple has worked in favour of apple for a long time. The iPhone completed 14 years this year and it still remains one the world's best selling smartphones and the biggest cashsow for Apple every year. Over the first half of the year, the latest period available, Apple said iPhone sales rose 58 percent to $87.5 billion over the same period a year earlier. This huge success of Apple is majorly because the have built a very huge loyal customer base and also because they have designed and built their products such that it makes customers really difficult to switch to other competitors like Samsung, OnePlus or Google. Having said that iPhone still remains among the best sellig smarhones in terms of design, user experience, built quality and innivation. During the entire event, Apple emphasized a lot on iPhone’s new “cinematic mode”. A camera technology that can automatically follow and focus on a subject, which results in videos that more closely resemble the professional movies. Throughout the video Apple emphasized on the advanced camera technology in the new iPhones is a reflection of their huge investment in Artificial Intelligence technology to compete with Google.
